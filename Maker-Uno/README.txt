#Maker Uno Buzzer
This is a buzzer sample for Cytron's Maker Uno, open the arduino file, and type "one", "two" or "three" into the serial monitor, the maker uno will play songs!

#Maker Uno Capacitive Sensing
Get 5 strips of copper or printable conductive ink and connect them just like this link: http://www.instructables.com/id/Capacitive-Sensing-for-Dummies/
When pressing the conductor the maker uno will play special notes!
